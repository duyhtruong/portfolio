Golden Hour

Live Demo Repository

In photography, 'Golden Hour' is the period of time just after the sun rises and sets over the horizon. Daylight during this time is diffused and even, with reduced shadows, and radiating with warm hues.


This minimal web app helps photographers take advantage of this lighting. Golden Hour takes a user inputted location in the form of city, zip code, address, or point of interest and outputs the time the sun rises and sets in local time. The app uses the Google Maps API to geolocate the user's input and the Sunrise Sunset API to retrieve the times in UTC. The time is then converted to the input's local time using the Google Time Zone API.
