Grub Burger Bar

Live Demo Repository

Restaurant chain Grub Burger Bar has made it's debut here in the Bay Area. The Texas born burger bar features an extensive menu including over-the-top burgers, boozy milkshakes, and creative sandwiches. For this project, I wanted to practice HTML, Less, and Javascript by creating a mock website for a restaurant I have been to.


The website features a navigation bar linked to sections on the page, a header with parallax scrolling, and custom borders.
